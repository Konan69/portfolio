## Overview

Galaxy Quest started as a growth experiment for a Web3 community, then evolved into a high-performance Telegram mini-app with gaming loops, loyalty rewards, and on-chain perks. I owned the full stack across React/Next.js, Postgres, and analytics so the product team could iterate rapidly on quests and monetization paths.

## Highlights

- **Engagement** – Custom rendering pipeline for Telegram Web Apps boosted session length by 40% and daily active users by 150% in the first two weeks.
- **Performance** – Optimized SSR and caching strategies cut API calls by 70% while keeping responses sub-200 ms for 10,000+ concurrent users.
- **Economy** – Integrated analytics mapped spending funnels in real time, informing price experiments that raised in-app purchases by 40%.
- **Platform Fit** – Hardened the backend to survive sudden viral spikes with PostgreSQL connection pooling, typed query builders, and autoscaling containers.

## Architecture

Next.js App Router powering the Telegram client, edge middleware for auth/session enforcement, a Go-based worker for scheduled drops, and PostgreSQL + Redis for state. Observability runs through OpenTelemetry traces piped into Grafana/Tempo for instant debugging.

## Impact

Galaxy Quest became the team's canonical template for future on-chain growth loops and continues to serve as a testbed for new quests, collectibles, and referral mechanics.
