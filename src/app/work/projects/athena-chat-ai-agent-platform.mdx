## Overview

Athena.chat is my ongoing effort to build an AI-native platform where specialized agents collaborate on business research, analysis, and workflow automation. The product ships with a React client, a Hono/Workers backend, and Cloudflare KV-based persistence so agents can spin up instantly with near-zero infrastructure overhead.

## Architecture

- **Task Decomposition Engine** – Parses complex prompts, breaks them into parallelizable subtasks, and routes them to the right agents using structured concurrency primitives.
- **Fan-out / Fan-in Coordination** – Multiple research agents work concurrently and contribute intermediate context to a coordinator that synthesizes final responses with reasoning traces.
- **RAG Orchestration Library** – Vector embeddings, semantic search, and retrieval pipelines tailored for contracts, marketing collateral, and product docs.
- **Scraping + Data Extraction** – Durable, rotating scrapers provide structured documents that agents can ingest safely with throttling, retries, and schema validation.

## Embeddable Assistants

A widget SDK lets customers drop assistants into their sites or apps with customizable UI components. Streaming responses keep conversations feeling real-time while the backend enforces rate limits and observability hooks.

## Impact

- Handles multi-agent workflows without human babysitting thanks to resilient planning/execution loops.
- Reduces research turnaround time from hours to minutes for GTM and recruiting teams.
- Enforces strong guardrails (auth, rate limits, audit logs) required for HR and finance deployments.

## Stack

React, Next.js App Router, Hono, Cloudflare Workers/KV, LangChain, LangGraph, PydanticAI, tRPC, PostgreSQL (Neon), Redis, Docker, OpenRouter/OpenAI models, Supabase Auth.
